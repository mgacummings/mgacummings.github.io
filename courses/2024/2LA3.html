Applications of Linear Algebra

Math 2LA3, Summer 2024

The course webpage is on Avenue, all information will be posted there.

Course outline

Schedule

  • Lectures: Mondays and Wednesdays 7–10pm
  • Office hours: TBD

See Avenue or Mosaic for the location. Our first class is Monday, June 24 — see you then! Office hours start on Wednesday, June 26.

We will cover the following topics:

  • Linear programming: geometric and simplex methods
  • Dynamical systems: basics of differential equations, eigenvalues, eigenvectors, diagonalization
  • Orthogonality: dot product, distance, projection, QR factorization, distance minimization, least squares, linear models
  • Constrained optimization: diagonalization of symmetric matrices, Spectral theorem, quadratic forms, singular value decomposition
  • Other topics as time permits. Exact topics can be picked during the term, but options include image processing and statistics, Markov chains, matrix games, and machine learning
