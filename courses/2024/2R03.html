Tutorial Structure

In tutorials, we will review definitions, build intuition, and work through sample problems in a way that will get you thinking about the material. It is no use me telling you exactly how to solve practice problems—the same way there is no use a personal trainer telling you how to perform exercises—it would not strengthen your proof-skill ability.
